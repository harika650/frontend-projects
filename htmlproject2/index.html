About Shawarma

Shawarma is a popular Middle Eastern street food made with thinly sliced cuts of meat, such as chicken, beef, lamb, or turkey, that are seasoned and slow-cooked on a rotating spit.

Ingredients

  • Thinly sliced meat (chicken, beef, lamb, or turkey)
  • Spices (garlic, paprika, turmeric, cumin, etc.)
  • Garlic sauce or tahini
  • Pita or flatbread
  • Fresh vegetables (lettuce, tomatoes, cucumbers, onions, pickles)

Nutritional Information (per serving)

Calories: 400-600 kcal, Protein: 25g, Fat: 20g, Carbohydrates: 40g

Popular Variations

Some popular shawarma variations include serving it with garlic sauce, hot sauce, or hummus, and choosing a wrap, sandwich, or plate.
